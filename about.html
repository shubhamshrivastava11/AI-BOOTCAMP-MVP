About Dwelliq

Dwelliq is an AI home styling advisor that turns a messy, undecided room into a clear plan of what to buy next, complete with three options for every piece.

This MVP focuses on the Triple‑Option engine: a budget‑anchored pick, an investment piece, and a local boutique option that respects your postcode and preferences.

You can return to the main experience any time on the home page.
