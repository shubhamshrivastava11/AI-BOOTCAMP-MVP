How Dwelliq works

01
Set your budget
Slide to your total room budget. We allocate across categories intelligently — 35% to the sofa, 15% to the rug, and so on.
02
Pick your style
Five visual directions with real room photography. Warm Mid-Century, Scandi Minimal, Earthy Organic, Modern Glam, Eclectic Maximalist.
03
Tell us your room
Room size, household type, how you use the space, and your postcode for local Option C. Four quick questions.
04
Get recommendations
Instant. Three options for every piece — within budget, best if flexible, and a boutique near you. Click to buy.
